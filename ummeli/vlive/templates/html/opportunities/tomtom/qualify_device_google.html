Android settings

For most phones running on Android, try the following steps:

  • Pull down the top menu and make sure GPS is ON
  • Open the Camera application
  • Find the Settings menu. This will open the 'Image settings' menu
  • Find the option that says GPS Tagand change it to ON
  • This should enable GPS tagging.

Known devices

  • Nexus (all)
  • Android (Samsung, LG, HTC, Huawei, Motorolla)

If your phone is not on the list, your device might not have GPS functions. Please consult your Phone Manual.
