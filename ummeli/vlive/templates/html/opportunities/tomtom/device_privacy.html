Privacy Settings

The most likely cause for our location detection not to work is that your browser has not been abled to provide location information.

The steps to enable location detection are different for different phones.

Common steps

  • In your browser, find Settings
  • Enable Location
  • This can also be found in the Privacy settings

    • Click here for help on taking GPS tagged pictures.
