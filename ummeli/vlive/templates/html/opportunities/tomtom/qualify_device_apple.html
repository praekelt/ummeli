Apple (iOS) settings

For most iOS devices, try the following steps:

  • You need to enable Location base services for the Camera
  • You can turn Location Services on or off at Settings > Privacy > Location Services > Camera
  • This should enable GPS tagging.

Notice

Please note that you can only upload images in the new iOS 6. Please make sure your version is 6 and above

Know devices

  • iPad 3G only (WiFi Only excluded)
  • iPhone (all)

If your phone is not on the list, your device might not have GPS functions. Please consult your Phone Manual.
