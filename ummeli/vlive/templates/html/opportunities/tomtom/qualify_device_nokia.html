Nokia settings

For most Nokia phones running the Symbian OS, try the following steps:

  • Open the Camera application
  • Find the Options → Settings menu. This will open the 'Image settings' menu
  • Find the option that says Save location info or Show GPS info (depending on Symbian version) and change it to YES or ON
  • This should enable GPS tagging.

Known devices

  • Nokia Lumia devices (most)
  • 808 PureView
  • C5, C6, C7
  • 500, 600, 700
  • N8, N9, N900, N85, N95, N97
  • E5, E6, E7
  • 5800, 5730, 5330, 5235

If your phone is not on the list, your device might not have GPS functions. Please consult your Phone Manual.
