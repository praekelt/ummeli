We are unable to verify what device you are using. Please consult your Phone Manual on how to enable GPS tagging on your phone. Please not that NOT all phones have GPS functionality.

The instructions below are guidelines on where you might find these settings on your phone.

  • Open the Camera application
  • Find the Options / Settings menu. This will open the 'Camera settings' menu
  • Find the option that says Save location info or Show GPS info or Enable GPS info
  • Change it to YES or ON or ENABLE
  • This should enable GPS tagging.
