BlackBerry settings

For most BlackBerry phones, try the following steps:

  • Open the Camera application
  • Find the Options → Settings menu. This will open the 'Image settings' menu
  • Find the option that says Save location info or Show GPS info and change it to YES or ON
  • This should enable GPS tagging.

Known devices

  • BlackBerry Bold
  • BlackBerry Curve
  • BlackBerry Storm

If your phone is not on the list, your device might not have GPS functions. Please consult your Phone Manual.
